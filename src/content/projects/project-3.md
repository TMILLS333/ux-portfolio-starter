## Overview

A mid-size fashion retailer was losing sales at checkout. The 7-step process was causing friction and cart abandonment.

## The Challenge

Analytics showed that 62% of users who added items to their cart never completed purchase. Most dropped off at step 3 (account creation).

## My Role

UX Designer — responsible for checkout flow redesign, A/B testing strategy, and design system updates.

## Process

1. **Analysis** — Mapped the existing 7-step flow and identified drop-off points
2. **Benchmarking** — Studied checkout patterns from 15 top e-commerce sites
3. **Redesign** — Consolidated into 3 steps with guest checkout option
4. **Testing** — Ran A/B test with 50,000 users over 2 weeks

## Results

- Cart abandonment reduced by 35%
- Average checkout time dropped from 4.5 to 1.8 minutes
- Guest checkout used by 58% of new customers
