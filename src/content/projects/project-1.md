## Overview

This project involved a complete redesign of a mobile banking app targeting Gen Z users. The goal was to make banking less intimidating and more intuitive.

## The Challenge

Young users found traditional banking apps overwhelming. Research showed they wanted simpler interfaces with clear explanations of financial concepts.

## My Role

Lead UX Designer — responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

## Process

1. **Research** — Interviewed 15 users aged 18-25 about their banking habits
2. **Synthesis** — Created affinity maps and user personas
3. **Design** — Developed wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma
4. **Testing** — Conducted 3 rounds of usability testing with 8 participants each

## Results

- 40% reduction in task completion time for common actions
- Net Promoter Score increased from 32 to 58
- App store rating improved from 3.2 to 4.6 stars
