## Overview

A healthcare network needed a patient portal that worked for everyone — from tech-savvy millennials to seniors with limited digital experience.

## The Challenge

The existing portal had a 67% abandonment rate. Patients couldn't find basic information and gave up, calling the office instead.

## My Role

UX Designer — focused on information architecture, accessibility compliance (WCAG AA), and interaction design.

## Process

1. **Audit** — Conducted a full accessibility and usability audit of the existing portal
2. **Research** — Observed 20 patients using the portal in a clinical setting
3. **Redesign** — Simplified the IA from 12 top-level sections to 4
4. **Validation** — Tested with assistive technology users

## Results

- Portal abandonment dropped from 67% to 12%
- Phone call volume to scheduling reduced by 45%
- WCAG AA compliance achieved across all pages
